Features

Everything You Need for Recovery

Built by researchers, designed for real-world physiotherapy workflows.

Guided Exercises

Browse and perform structured exercise routines designed by physiotherapists. Each exercise comes with clear instructions and progress tracking.

VR Device Integration

Connect VR glasses via UDP for an immersive rehabilitation experience. Real-time device communication makes sessions engaging and effective.

Physio & Client Roles

Dedicated dashboards for physiotherapists and clients. Therapists assign exercises, clients perform and report — seamlessly connected.

Local Device Discovery

Automatic WiFi-based device discovery finds nearby sensors and VR hardware without manual configuration.

Session History

Every completed session is recorded. Review past performance, track improvement over time, and export data for analysis.

Secure Cloud Sync

Powered by Supabase with email verification, your data stays safe. Import and export session data whenever you need.

How It Works

Get Moving in 3 Steps

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email, verify it, and choose your role — Client or Physiotherapist.

2

Connect Devices

Pair your phone with VR glasses or other sensors over your local WiFi network automatically.

3

Start Exercising

Follow guided exercise routines, track your session, and watch your progress over time.
